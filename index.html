01 / About

Collaborative AI: Secure & Robust by Design

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Jodhpur (thesis submitted), working under the guidance of Dr. Deepak Mishra. My research focuses on the intersection of federated learning, privacy-preserving machine learning, and medical image analysis — designing architectures that enable collaborative model training without compromising patient confidentiality.

My doctoral work introduces four original Federated Learning frameworks to systematically resolve core distributed challenges: communication efficiency (BAFL), label scarcity (FLAME), Byzantine-attack robustness (AR2FL), and client-side machine unlearning (SPU) for regulatory compliance.

Beyond core distributed AI, I collaborate on sponsored defense research, developing robust, real-time machine learning pipelines for radar sensing and moving-target detection.
